## Frameworks (Essential Info Only)

### Structured Clarity
- **CRISPE**: Clarity, Relevance, Iteration, Specificity, Parameters, Examples. For: technical docs, strategic planning. Complexity: Medium
- **RELIC**: Role, Emphasis, Limitation, Information, Challenge. For: content creation, strategic planning. Complexity: Medium
- **RTF**: Request, Task, Format. For: data retrieval, simple requests. Complexity: Low

### Iterative Refinement
- **RACEF**: Rephrase, Append, Contextualize, Examples, Follow-Up. For: brainstorming, iterative problem-solving. Complexity: Medium
- **Chain of Destiny**: Baseline + Feedback loops. For: projects prioritising quality. Complexity: High

### Decision-Making & Prioritisation
- **RICE**: Reach, Impact, Confidence, Effort. For: feature prioritisation, project selection. Complexity: Low
- **SMART**: Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound. For: goal-setting. Complexity: Low
- **COAST**: Challenge, Objective, Actions, Strategy, Tactics. For: project management. Complexity: Medium
- **Pros and Cons**: Benefits vs Drawbacks. For: decision-making. Complexity: Low

### Analytical & Problem-Solving
- **Chain of Thought**: Intro, Breakdown, Logical Progression, Conclusion. For: complex reasoning. Complexity: High
- **Tree of Thought**: Nodes, Edges, Outcomes. For: complex problem-solving, scenario planning. Complexity: High
- **FOCUS**: Focus areas, Prioritisation, Resources. For: goal-setting and prioritisation. Complexity: Medium
- **Six Thinking Hats**: White (facts), Red (emotions), Black (risks), Yellow (benefits), Green (creativity), Blue (process). For: multi-perspective analysis. Complexity: Medium

### Storytelling & Narrative
- **BAB**: Before, After, Bridge. For: marketing, persuasion. Complexity: Low
- **CAR**: Context, Action, Result. For: case studies, interviews. Complexity: Low
- **PAR**: Problem, Action, Result. For: success stories. Complexity: Low
- **STAR**: Situation, Task, Action, Result. For: interviews, case studies. Complexity: Low
- **Challenge-Solution-Benefit**: For: marketing, product development. Complexity: Low

### Content Creation
- **BLOG**: Background, Logic, Outline, Goal. For: blog posts, articles. Complexity: Low
- **APE**: Audience, Purpose, Execution. For: content marketing. Complexity: Low
- **TAG**: Topic, Audience, Goal. For: content marketing. Complexity: Low
- **4S Method**: Structure, Style, Substance, Speed. For: digital marketing. Complexity: Low
- **Hamburger**: Intro, Body, Conclusion. For: blogs, articles. Complexity: Low

### Creative & Innovation
- **SCAMPER**: Substitute, Combine, Adapt, Modify, Put to another use, Eliminate, Reverse. For: innovation, product development. Complexity: Medium
- **HMW**: "How might we..." questions. For: design thinking, brainstorming. Complexity: Low
- **Imagine**: Future scenario visioning. For: strategic planning. Complexity: Low
- **What If**: Hypothetical scenarios. For: creative problem-solving. Complexity: Low
- **SPARK**: Situation, Problem, Aspiration, Result, Kismet. For: product development, marketing. Complexity: Medium

### Educational & Learning
- **Bloom's Taxonomy**: Remember → Understand → Apply → Analyze → Evaluate → Create. For: educational content. Complexity: Medium
- **ELI5**: Explain Like I'm 5. For: simplifying complex concepts. Complexity: Low
- **Help Me Understand**: Comprehension-focused. For: customer support, education. Complexity: Low
- **TQA**: Thematic, Question, Answer. For: e-learning. Complexity: Low
- **Socratic Method**: Progressive questioning. For: education, critical analysis. Complexity: Medium

### Communication & Engagement
- **RACE**: Reach, Act, Convert, Engage. For: marketing campaigns. Complexity: Medium
- **ERA**: Engage, React, Act. For: marketing, engagement. Complexity: Low
- **CARE**: Compassion, Awareness, Response, Engagement. For: customer service. Complexity: Medium

### Strategic Analysis
- **3Cs**: Company, Customer, Competitor. For: market analysis. Complexity: Medium
- **GOPA**: Goals, Obstacles, Plans, Actions. For: goal setting, problem-solving. Complexity: Low

### Feedback & Improvement
- **RISE**: Reflect, Inquire, Suggest, Elevate. For: performance management. Complexity: Medium
- **ROSES**: Recognize, Observe, Strategize, Execute, Study. For: complex projects. Complexity: Medium
- **PEE**: Point, Evidence, Explanation. For: academic writing, critiques. Complexity: Low

### Advanced Structured
- **RASCEF**: Role, Action, Steps, Context, Examples, Format. For: technical documentation. Complexity: Medium
- **RHODES**: Role, Objective, Details, Examples, Sense Check. For: creative content, marketing. Complexity: Medium
- **RISEN**: Role, Input, Steps, Expectation, Novelty. For: research, innovation. Complexity: Medium
- **GRADE**: Goal, Request, Action, Details, Example. For: project management. Complexity: Medium
- **TRACI**: Task, Role, Audience, Create, Intent. For: marketing, education. Complexity: Medium
- **RODES**: Role, Objective, Details, Examples, Sense Check. For: educational content. Complexity: Medium
- **CIDI**: Context, Instructions, Details, Input. For: project management. Complexity: Medium

### Argumentation
- **TRACE**: Topic, Reason, Audience, Counterargument, Evidence. For: debate, persuasive writing. Complexity: Medium
- **SPAR**: Situation, Problem, Action, Result. For: case studies. Complexity: Low
- **PROMPT**: Precision, Relevance, Objectivity, Method, Provenance, Timeliness. For: research, journalism. Complexity: Medium

### Specialised
- **SPEAR**: Start, Provide, Explain, Ask, Rinse & Repeat. For: everyday tasks. Complexity: Low
- **Few-Shot**: Task + Demonstrations + Query. For: classification, creative content. Complexity: Low
- **Zero-Shot**: Direct instruction without examples. For: translation, factual queries. Complexity: Low
- **ORID**: Objective, Reflective, Interpretive, Decisional. For: group discussions, coaching. Complexity: Medium
- **PAUSE**: Prepare, Assess, Uncover, Synthesize, Execute. For: management decisions. Complexity: Medium
- **Elicitation**: Structured information extraction. For: research, interviews. Complexity: Medium

### Visual & Dialogue
- **Atomic Prompting**: Detailed visual specs. For: image generation (Midjourney, DALL-E). Complexity: Medium
- **Five Ws and One H**: Who, What, When, Where, Why, How. For: journalism, research. Complexity: Low

## Selection Algorithm

1. Classify task into category
2. Retrieve candidate frameworks from mapping
3. Prefer "Primary" frameworks
4. Match complexity:
   - Simple task → Low complexity framework
   - Moderate task → Low or Medium complexity
   - Complex task → Medium or High complexity
5. Select ONE framework for construction
6. Note alternatives

Complexity criteria:
- **Simple**: Single-step, clear output, minimal context
- **Moderate**: Multi-step, some ambiguity, needs context
- **Complex**: Multi-faceted, significant ambiguity, extensive context, multiple stakeholders
