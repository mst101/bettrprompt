# AI Prompt Framework Selection Matrix

**Purpose**: Quick reference for selecting the optimal prompt engineering framework based on task type and personality.

## Framework Categories Overview

1. **Structured Clarity** - Precise, organised outputs (CRISPE, RELIC, RTF)
2. **Iterative Refinement** - Progressive improvement (RACEF, Chain of Destiny)
3. **Decision-Making** - Prioritisation and evaluation (RICE, SMART, COAST)
4. **Reasoning & Analysis** - Logical problem-solving (Chain of Thought, Tree of Thought)
5. **Persuasive & Marketing** - Influence and conversion (BAB, CAR-PAR-STAR)
6. **Creative Exploration** - Ideation and innovation (SCAMPER, How Might We, What If)
7. **Educational** - Learning and explanation (Bloom's Taxonomy, ELI5)
8. **Customer-Focused** - User engagement (RACE, ERA, CARE)
9. **Strategic Analysis** - Business and planning (3Cs, 4S, GOPA)
10. **Structured Reasoning** - Systematic approaches (RISE, ROSES, PEE, SPEAR)

## Selection Criteria

1. **Task Complexity**: Simple → RTF, Zero-Shot | Complex → Chain of Thought, CRISPE, Atomic Prompting
2. **Audience**: Technical → CRISPE, CIDI | General → ELI5, Help Me Understand | Marketing → BAB, TAG
3. **Output Type**: Analysis → Chain of Thought, TQA | Content → BLOG, APE | Strategy → COAST, GOPA
4. **Iteration Needs**: One-shot → GRADE, RTF | Iterative → RACEF, Chain of Destiny, SPEAR
5. **Decision Focus**: Evaluation → RICE, Pros & Cons | Planning → SMART, COAST
6. **Creativity Level**: Structured → CRISPE, SMART | Creative → SCAMPER, How Might We, Imagine
7. **Personality Alignment**: Match framework strengths to personality preferences
