# AI Prompt Builder: Audience Matrix & USPs

## 1️⃣ Clarify the Concept

Mark's idea --- *a better AI prompt builder for novice AI users* ---
sits at the intersection of **AI adoption** and **user competency**.

So your quadrant can help position who the product helps most and how
messaging can shift as users evolve.

**Axes:** - **X-axis → AI Adoption Level** (from *Low adoption* → *High
adoption*) - **Y-axis → User Competency** (from *Low skill* → *High
skill*)

## 3️⃣ Identify Target Segments

For launch:\
🎯 **Primary focus:** Quadrant A & B --- the *AI-curious but uncertain
users.*

These users value: - *Confidence* ("I know how to ask AI properly")\
- *Speed* ("I don't have to spend time crafting prompts")\
- *Clarity* ("It speaks my language, not tech jargon")

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 5️⃣ Unique Selling Points (USPs) for the Prompt Builder

Once we know Quadrant A & B are your early market, your USPs could
include:

1.  **Prompt Coaching, Not Prompt Dumping** -- it *teaches* while it
    builds.\
2.  **Smart Prompt Translator** -- converts plain English goals into
    optimized AI prompts.\
3.  **Adaptive Guidance** -- improves suggestions as it learns your
    tone, purpose, and tools.\
4.  **Confidence Mode** -- shows *why* each part of the prompt works (so
    users learn).\
5.  **One-Click Output Preview** -- test your prompt instantly in your
    preferred AI (ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot, etc.).\
6.  **Templates for Everyday Scenarios** -- pre-built use cases: job
    search, emails, content, research, etc.\
7.  **Gamified Progression** -- earn "Prompt Levels" to motivate skill
    growth.\
8.  **Team Mode** -- shared prompt library for colleagues or
    communities.
