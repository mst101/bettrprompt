# Project Summary

AI Buddy is an intelligent assistant that creates optimised AI prompts customised to users' personality types and tasks.

## Purpose

Bridge psychology with AI prompt engineering, helping users get more contextually relevant and personalised outputs from AI systems.

## What It Does

AI Buddy creates optimised AI prompts customised to your personality type and the specific task you're trying to accomplish. By understanding your personality traits (based on 16personalities.com types) and applying established prompt frameworks, tailored to the task at hand, it generates high-quality prompts that will produce better results from AI systems.

## How It Works

This system follows a personalised workflow:

1. **Collects your personality information** - Select your 16personalities.com personality type and optionally enter your trait percentages
2. **Understands your task** - Describe what you're trying to accomplish
3. **Selects appropriate frameworks** - Chooses the best prompt framework (SMART, RICE, COAST, etc.) for your task
4. **Adopts a complementary role** - Either amplifies your personality traits or balances them with complementary traits
5. **Asks clarifying questions** - Tailored to your personality type and using the selected framework
6. **Creates an optimised prompt** - Generates a prompt engineered for better results

## Key Features

- **Personality-based customisation**: Leverages 16Personalities framework (INTJ-A, ENFP-T, etc.)
- **Framework selection**: Automatically chooses from SMART, RICE, COAST, and other prompt frameworks
- **Interactive refinement**: Asks clarifying questions to understand your task better
- **Trait percentages**: Optional detailed personality input for even more precise prompts
- **Prompt history**: Save and review all your optimised prompts

## How to Use

1. Visit the website/app
2. Select your personality type from the dropdown menu
3. Optionally enter your trait percentages (e.g., "I - 65%, N - 64%, T - 84%, P - 57%, A - 84%")
4. Describe your task in detail
5. Engage with the clarifying questions to refine understanding
6. Receive your personalised, optimised prompt

## Technical Overview

It combines psychological insight (16Personalities and similar frameworks) with prompt frameworks (SMART, RICCE, COAST) to produce tailored AI interactions.
